Featured Delaware Beach Towns

Use these town profiles as a starting point for planning your stay, choosing a rental, building an itinerary, or deciding where to spend a beach day.

Rehoboth Beach
Classic Boardwalk Restaurants

Best for a classic Delaware beach experience.

Rehoboth is one of Delaware’s most popular beach towns, known for its boardwalk, restaurants, shops, family-friendly attractions, and full vacation energy.

Best for families, first-time visitors, boardwalk trips, shopping, dining, and people who want a complete beach-town experience.

Plan a Rehoboth Trip
Dewey Beach
Nightlife Music Groups

Best for social weekends and beach-bar energy.

Dewey has a younger, livelier feel with beach days, nightlife, live music, bayside sunsets, and a more energetic social scene.

Best for nightlife, groups, music, beach bars, weekend trips, and visitors who want more movement after dark.

Watch Beach Videos
Bethany Beach
Family Quiet Rental Week

Best for a calmer family beach week.

Bethany is quieter and more relaxed, with a family-friendly atmosphere, a smaller boardwalk, and a calmer coastal pace.

Best for families, peaceful beach weeks, slower vacations, cozy rental homes, and travelers who want less nightlife and more ease.

Upgrade Your Rental
Lewes
Historic Cape Henlopen Scenic

Best for charm, history, and nature.

Lewes blends beach access with history, restaurants, shops, Cape Henlopen State Park, and a more classic coastal-town feel.

Best for history, nature, Cape Henlopen, quieter stays, scenic day trips, and visitors who want a less crowded coastal experience.

Fenwick Island
Quiet Family South Coast

Best for quieter stays near the Maryland line.

Fenwick Island offers a quieter beach escape with access to family attractions, restaurants, and nearby Ocean City energy.

Best for quieter beach stays, families, and travelers who want access to both Delaware and Maryland beach options.

Plan a Fenwick Stay
South Bethany
Rental Homes Quiet Laid Back

Best for rental homes and peaceful routines.

South Bethany and nearby coastal neighborhoods are great for travelers who want a quieter rental-home experience close to the beach without the heavier crowds.

Best for family weeks, peaceful stays, slow mornings, and laid-back beach routines.
