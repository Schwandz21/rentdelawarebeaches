Find the Delaware Beach Town That Fits Your Trip

Delaware’s beach towns are close together, but each one has a different feel. Some are better for nightlife, some are better for families, some are quieter, and some are ideal for shopping, restaurants, history, or a classic boardwalk day.

Use this guide as a starting point when planning a Delaware beach vacation, rental stay, day trip, weekend getaway, or coastal home search.

Featured Delaware Beach Towns

Rehoboth Beach

Rehoboth is one of Delaware’s most popular beach towns, known for its boardwalk, restaurants, shops, family-friendly attractions, and classic beach-town energy.

Best for: families, boardwalk trips, dining, shopping, and first-time Delaware beach visitors.

Dewey Beach

Dewey has a younger, livelier feel with beach days, nightlife, live music, bayside sunsets, and a more energetic social scene.

Best for: nightlife, music, groups, beach bars, and weekend energy.

Bethany Beach

Bethany is quieter and more relaxed, with a family-friendly atmosphere, a smaller boardwalk, and a calmer coastal pace.

Best for: families, peaceful beach weeks, slower vacations, and cozy rental homes.

Lewes

Lewes blends beach access with history, charm, restaurants, shops, Cape Henlopen State Park, and a more classic coastal-town feel.

Best for: history, nature, Cape Henlopen, quieter stays, and scenic day trips.

Fenwick Island

Fenwick Island sits near the Maryland line and offers a quieter beach escape with access to family attractions, restaurants, and nearby Ocean City energy.

Best for: quieter beach stays, families, and travelers who want access to both Delaware and Maryland beaches.

South Bethany & Nearby Coastal Areas

South Bethany and nearby coastal neighborhoods are great for travelers who want a quieter rental-home experience close to the beach without the heavier crowds.

Best for: rental homes, family weeks, peaceful stays, and laid-back beach routines.

Quick Pick Guide

Best classic beach-town experience: Rehoboth Beach

Best nightlife: Dewey Beach

Best quiet family feel: Bethany Beach

Best history and nature mix: Lewes

Best quiet southern Delaware beach option: Fenwick Island / South Bethany
