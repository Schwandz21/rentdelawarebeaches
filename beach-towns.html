Quick Guide

Choose the town that matches the trip.

Rent Delaware Beaches is designed to help visitors, homeowners, and rental owners quickly understand the difference between Delaware beach towns without digging through cluttered travel pages.

Classic Beach Energy

Rehoboth & Dewey Beach.

Boardwalk + dining

Rehoboth Beach

Rehoboth is one of Delaware’s most recognizable beach towns, with boardwalk energy, restaurants, shops, family attractions, and a classic beach-town feel.

  • Good for families and first-time visitors
  • Strong restaurant and shopping scene
  • Classic boardwalk experience
Nightlife + beach days

Dewey Beach

Dewey is compact, lively, and known for beach days, music, nightlife, bay access, and a more energetic coastal atmosphere.

  • Good for groups and nightlife
  • Beach and bay access nearby
  • More energetic than quiet beach towns
Best fit

Who should stay here?

Choose this area if you want restaurants, walkability, activity, nightlife, and the feeling of being close to the center of the action.

  • Visitors who like busier beach towns
  • Rental owners serving high-demand areas
  • People who want a full beach-town experience
Historic + Natural

Lewes & Cape Henlopen.

Historic charm

Lewes

Lewes has a quieter, historic feel with local shops, restaurants, bay views, and a different pace than the busier resort towns.

  • Good for slower trips
  • Historic town center feel
  • Restaurants, shops, and bay access
Trails + nature

Cape Henlopen

Cape Henlopen adds trails, nature, beaches, biking, and scenic outdoor experiences close to Lewes.

  • Good for biking and trails
  • Nature-focused beach days
  • Great for outdoor-focused visitors
Best fit

Who should stay here?

Choose Lewes if you want a calmer, more historic, more nature-oriented coastal stay.

  • Visitors who like quiet charm
  • Families who want less chaos
  • Outdoor and nature-focused trips
Relaxed Family Beach

Bethany, South Bethany & Fenwick.

Family-friendly

Bethany Beach

Bethany is known for a calmer family beach feel, a compact town center, and a more relaxed pace than Rehoboth or Dewey.

  • Good for family trips
  • Relaxed beach-house atmosphere
  • Less nightlife-driven
Quiet rental feel

South Bethany

South Bethany has a more residential feel and is often a strong fit for people who want a quieter beach-house setting.

  • Good for slower beach weeks
  • Residential rental atmosphere
  • Better for calm than crowds
Coastal edge

Fenwick Island

Fenwick sits near the southern end of Delaware’s beach towns and works well for relaxed beach trips, family stops, and access toward Ocean City.

  • Good for families and quieter stays
  • Close to more southern beach activity
  • Great for laid-back coastal rentals
