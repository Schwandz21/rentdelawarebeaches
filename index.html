Featured Delaware Beach Towns

Rehoboth Beach

Boardwalk attractions, beach rentals, restaurants, nightlife, and one of Delaware’s most popular coastal destinations.

Best For: Families, boardwalk fun, food, shopping, and classic beach atmosphere.

Popular Attractions: Funland, Rehoboth Boardwalk, Dogfish Head Brewery, Cape Henlopen nearby.

Dewey Beach

Known for nightlife, live music, beach bars, and younger beach crowds.

Best For: Nightlife, live music, bars, social atmosphere, and younger vacationers.

Popular Attractions: The Starboard, Bottle & Cork, Dewey Beach nightlife, and nearby Rehoboth access.

Bethany Beach

A quieter family-oriented beach town known for peaceful vacations and relaxing coastal vibes.

Best For: Families, relaxing beach trips, peaceful vacations, and quiet boardwalk walks.

Popular Attractions: Bethany Beach Boardwalk, family beach rentals, local restaurants, and coastal relaxation.

Lewes

Historic coastal town with waterfront charm, seafood restaurants, and Cape Henlopen access.

Best For: History lovers, seafood dining, quiet coastal experiences, and outdoor activities.

Popular Attractions: Cape Henlopen State Park, Lewes Ferry, downtown Lewes shops, and fishing charters.

Fenwick Island

A quieter coastal destination offering family beaches and easy access to Ocean City attractions.

Best For: Family vacations, quieter beaches, fishing, and relaxing coastal getaways.

Popular Attractions: Fenwick Island State Park, mini golf, water activities, and nearby Ocean City entertainment.
