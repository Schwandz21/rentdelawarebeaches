Future Community Features

This page is built as a foundation. The first version is simple, but the structure is ready for bigger features as the site grows.

Phase 1

Email Submissions

People send questions, recommendations, tips, clips, and product ideas by email. You curate the best ones into site content.

Phase 2

Google Form or Simple Submission Form

Add a clean form for structured submissions: town, category, recommendation, question, photo, video link, or business name.

Phase 3

Featured Community Posts

Turn the best questions into blog posts, quick guides, video scripts, restaurant roundups, and town-specific recommendations.

Phase 4

Comments, Ratings & Discussions

Later, add real comments, ratings, town threads, restaurant notes, rental-owner advice, and local business profiles.

This Is How the Site Becomes Alive
